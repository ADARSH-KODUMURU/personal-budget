What is a Personal Budget?

A personal budget is a financial plan that outlines your income and expenses over a specific period, typically a month.

It helps you track where your money is coming from and where it's going.

The primary goal of a personal budget is to ensure that you live within your means and allocate your resources wisely to meet your financial objectives.

Why Create a Personal Budget?

1.Financial Awareness: A budget provides a clear picture of your financial situation, helping you understand your spending habits and identify areas where you can save money.

2.Goal Achievement: Budgets help you allocate funds for specific financial goals, such as saving for a vacation, buying a home, paying off debt, or building an emergency fund.

3.Debt Management: By tracking expenses and prioritizing debt payments, you can work towards reducing and eventually eliminating high-interest debts.

4.Emergency Preparedness: A budget allows you to set aside money for unexpected expenses, ensuring you have a financial safety net in case of emergencies.

5.Peace of Mind: Knowing you have control over your finances and are working toward your goals can reduce financial stress and provide peace of mind.
